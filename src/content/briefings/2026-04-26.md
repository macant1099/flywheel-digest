# Flywheel Affiliate Daily

## The Lead

**Anthropic built a marketplace where AI agents buy and sell for real money — and the results should worry every affiliate program manager.**

On Friday, Anthropic published the results of Project Deal, an internal experiment where 69 employees were each given $100 and represented by Claude agents in a classified-ad marketplace. The agents negotiated on behalf of both buyers and sellers, striking 186 deals worth over $4,000 in actual goods and cash.

The headline finding: users represented by more advanced models got "objectively better outcomes" — better prices, better deals. But the people on the losing side didn't notice. Anthropic flagged this as an emerging "agent quality gap," where the sophistication of your AI proxy directly determines your commercial results, and you may never realize you're being outmatched.

For the affiliate industry, this isn't hypothetical anymore. If AI agents are negotiating purchases, comparing prices, and executing transactions autonomously, then the affiliate layer that connects shoppers to merchants needs to speak agent-to-agent, not just human-to-browser. Commission structures, attribution models, and partner onboarding all need to account for a world where the "customer" is software. The vendors who adapt their affiliate programs for agentic commerce first will capture the traffic that's already surging — and the ones who don't may never see what they lost.

## What's Moving

- **AI retail traffic up 393% YoY.** Adobe Analytics data for Q1 2026 shows AI-referred visits to U.S. retail sites now convert 42% better than traditional sources like paid search and email — a complete reversal from March 2025, when AI traffic converted 38% worse. Revenue per visit is 37% higher. The channel has gone from curiosity to performance leader in twelve months.

- **Meta rolls out affiliate tools across Instagram and Facebook.** Shoppable Reels, creator product tagging, and affiliate banners are now live, with Amazon, eBay, Shopee, Temu, and Mercado Libre as launch partners. Advertisers say it's a "first step" — useful for beauty and wellness, but adoption across other verticals remains slow.

- **FTC targets digital ad ecosystem collusion.** The Commission, joined by a coalition of states, filed action against major advertising agencies for allegedly distorting competition in digital advertising. For affiliate marketers, this signals continued regulatory pressure on the intermediary layer between brands and consumers.

- **Ulta Beauty launches Gemini-powered AI shopping assistant.** Built with Google's Gemini Enterprise, "Ulta AI" handles product discovery and purchase across Google surfaces — another major retailer betting on agentic commerce as a primary channel.

- **PayPal Honey affiliate lawsuit moves forward.** After a federal judge initially dismissed the case, plaintiffs filed an amended complaint in early 2026 alleging Honey's browser extension diverted affiliate commissions. The outcome could reshape how coupon and loyalty tools interact with affiliate attribution.

## Partner Landscape

**Profile: AI Shopping Agents as Affiliate Partners**

They don't have Instagram followings or email lists, but AI shopping agents are rapidly becoming the affiliate channel that matters most. Adobe's Q1 data confirms it: AI-referred traffic doesn't just visit — it buys, and it buys more per session than human-originated traffic.

The landscape is fragmenting fast. Google's Gemini surfaces product recommendations inside Search and Shopping. Perplexity's shopping agent browses, compares, and attempts checkout autonomously (though Amazon just won a preliminary injunction blocking its purchases). Meta is embedding AI sales agents directly into Business Suite with checkout capability.

For vendors, the playbook is shifting. Traditional affiliate onboarding assumed a human partner who writes content, posts links, and drives clicks. AI agents don't do any of that — they read product feeds, evaluate structured data, and route transactions programmatically. Vendors who invest in clean product feeds, competitive commission structures, and machine-readable program terms are the ones these agents will favor.

## The AI Angle

Anthropic's Project Deal is the first public proof that AI agents can handle real commercial negotiations end-to-end, and that model quality creates measurable economic advantage. Combined with Adobe's data showing AI traffic now outperforms human traffic on conversion and revenue, the signal is clear: agentic commerce isn't a 2027 problem. It's a Q2 2026 reality that affiliate programs need to build for today.

## One Thing to Do Today

Audit your product data feed. AI shopping agents make purchase decisions based on structured data — titles, descriptions, prices, availability, and images. Pull up your feed and check: are product titles clear and specific? Are descriptions complete? Is pricing current? A clean feed is the new SEO for the agentic commerce era, and ten minutes of cleanup can determine whether AI agents surface your products or skip them entirely.
