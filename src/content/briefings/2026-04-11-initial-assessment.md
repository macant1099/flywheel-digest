# Initial AFARE Assessments Complete

The Flywheel Affiliate Intelligence Engine has completed its first two AFARE (Affiliate Readiness Evaluation) vendor scorecards.

## Anker — 64/100 (Developing)

Anker's affiliate program demonstrates maturity built on strong infrastructure. Key findings:

- **Above-median 8% commission** on Impact, exceeding the consumer electronics category median of 5%
- **Best-in-class Shopify Plus** platform with 50+ global storefronts and headless architecture
- **Premium editorial coverage** from Tom's Guide, TechRadar, CNN Underscored, Engadget, and Wirecutter
- **Gap: No AI agent affiliate strategy** — all agent-driven revenue routes through Amazon Associates
- **Gap: No membership commerce model** — purely transactional, limiting LiveChannel integration potential

Path to 75+ (Established): Launch AI agent affiliate tier, build membership offering, accelerate payment terms.

## Priority Bicycles — 44/100 (Early)

Priority Bicycles punches above its weight as a small DTC brand with thoughtful partnership architecture:

- **Four distinct partner programs** (affiliate, content creator, referral, dealer) — rare sophistication for DTC scale
- **Strong program discoverability** (4/4) — appears in 5+ "best bicycle affiliate programs" roundups
- **$550+ average order value** yields ~$27.50 per affiliate sale despite median commission rate
- **Gap: No major network presence** — absent from CJ, Awin, Impact, and Rakuten
- **Gap: 21-day cookie** is below the 30-day category standard

Path to 60+ (Developing): Launch on Impact or CJ, extend cookie to 30-45 days, introduce tiered commissions.
