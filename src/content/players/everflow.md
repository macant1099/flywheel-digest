## Overview

Everflow is a partner marketing platform designed for brands, agencies, and networks that want granular control over their partnership tracking and analytics. Founded in May 2016 in San Diego, California, by Sam Darawish (CEO), Kanwar Dhaliwal (COO), and Jonathan Blais (CTO), the company was built on experience gained from operating Moolah Media, one of the earliest mobile affiliate networks.

What makes Everflow notable is that it is a profitable, bootstrapped company that has grown entirely through customer satisfaction and referrals rather than venture capital. In 2025, Everflow processed $4.3 billion in partner-tracked revenue for its global customers, a 19% year-over-year increase. The platform serves 1,200+ brands, including JG Wentworth, Moen, and Golden Hippo, with a team of 200+ professionals worldwide.

## How It Works

Everflow operates as a tracking and analytics platform (not a network) that brands use to manage their partnerships:

1. **Multi-Channel Tracking**: Everflow tracks all partnership types including affiliates, influencers, strategic partners, referral programs, and media buyers. The platform supports both click-based and direct linking attribution.
2. **Real-Time Analytics**: Performance data is available in real time, with granular placement-level tracking that goes beyond simple click-and-conversion reporting.
3. **Fraud Prevention**: Built-in fraud detection tools identify and block suspicious traffic before it reaches advertisers.
4. **Automated Optimization**: Rules-based automation for commission adjustments, partner tier management, and performance alerts.
5. **Payment Processing**: Integrated payment processing for partner commissions across multiple currencies and methods.

## Key Products & Services

### Partner Marketing Platform
The core product for tracking, managing, and optimizing all types of marketing partnerships in one place.

### Traffic Health (2025)
A first-of-its-kind domain health and IP blacklist monitoring tool backed by Google Threat Intelligence, helping brands identify and prevent traffic fraud before it impacts campaigns.

### Customer Value
A feature that moves beyond single-conversion tracking to capture a user's entire lifetime value (LTV), enabling automated high-value bonuses and payouts based on long-term customer contribution.

### Flowchart Visualization
Visual mapping of the complete customer journey, identifying crucial touchpoints and enabling faster troubleshooting of tracking issues.

### Direct Linking
Unique attribution that tracks similar to Google Analytics, providing organic-feeling user experiences while maintaining full partner attribution.

### Featured Marketplace Partners
A curated directory of partners across various verticals, helping brands discover and connect with relevant affiliates and publishers.

## Pricing & Commission Model

Everflow uses a straightforward SaaS pricing model:

- **Core Plan**: Starting at $750/month for up to 25,000 clicks, includes unlimited partners, real-time tracking, and core features.
- **Custom/Enterprise**: Scaling pricing based on click volume, advanced features, and support level. Custom pricing for high-volume programs.
- **No Commission Override**: Like other SaaS platforms, Everflow does not take a percentage of partner commissions.

The pricing model is transparent and predictable, making it easy for brands to budget their partnership program costs.

## Strengths

- **Placement-Level Tracking**: Everflow's granular tracking at the placement level (not just partner level) provides deeper performance insights than most competitors.
- **Bootstrapped Profitability**: No venture capital pressure means the company can focus on product quality and customer satisfaction rather than growth-at-all-costs metrics.
- **Fraud Prevention**: Traffic Health, backed by Google Threat Intelligence, provides industry-leading fraud detection and prevention.
- **Multi-Channel Flexibility**: Tracks affiliates, influencers, strategic partners, and media buyers under one platform, avoiding tool fragmentation.
- **Customer LTV Tracking**: The Customer Value feature enables sophisticated commission models based on long-term customer contribution, not just initial conversions.

## Weaknesses

- **Not a Network**: Everflow provides the tracking platform but not the publisher relationships. Brands need to recruit partners independently or through other networks.
- **Less Brand Recognition**: Compared to Impact.com or CJ, Everflow has lower brand awareness, particularly among enterprise brands.
- **Smaller Ecosystem**: Without a built-in marketplace of 300,000+ partners, brands starting from scratch face a cold-start problem for partner recruitment.
- **Technical Setup**: The platform's depth means more complex initial setup compared to simpler plug-and-play solutions.

## Market Position & Competitive Context

Everflow occupies a distinct niche as a tracking platform (not a network or full SaaS suite):

- **Everflow vs. Impact.com/Partnerize**: These are full partnership management platforms with marketplaces, contracting, and payment tools. Everflow focuses on tracking and analytics depth. Brands may use Everflow underneath or alongside network participation.
- **Everflow vs. CJ/Awin**: Networks provide publisher relationships; Everflow provides tracking infrastructure. They can coexist when brands want network reach plus granular tracking.
- **Everflow vs. HasOffers/TUNE**: In the mobile and performance marketing tracking space, Everflow competes with legacy platforms but differentiates through placement-level tracking and modern architecture.

Everflow's bootstrapped, profitable status is notable in a landscape dominated by venture-funded competitors. This independence means the company can prioritize product quality over growth metrics.

## Ideal Use Cases

Everflow is particularly well-suited for:

- **Performance Marketing Agencies**: Managing multiple client programs with granular tracking and reporting across diverse partner types.
- **Affiliate Networks**: Smaller or mid-market affiliate networks that need robust tracking infrastructure without building it themselves.
- **Multi-Channel Brands**: Brands running affiliate, influencer, media buying, and strategic partner programs that want unified tracking across all channels.
- **Fraud-Sensitive Verticals**: Industries like financial services, insurance, and lead generation where fraud prevention and traffic quality monitoring are critical.
- **LTV-Focused Programs**: Brands that want to commission based on long-term customer value rather than just initial conversions.

## Vendor Takeaway

Everflow is the right choice for brands that want maximum control and granularity over their partnership tracking without the constraints of operating within a traditional network. It is particularly well-suited for performance marketers, agencies managing multiple client programs, and brands with existing partner relationships that need sophisticated tracking infrastructure. For Flywheel vendors, Everflow is most relevant as a complement to network presence rather than a replacement: use CJ or Awin for publisher discovery and recruitment, and Everflow for advanced tracking and optimization. The platform's Customer Value feature is especially relevant for vendors focused on lifetime customer value rather than just initial acquisition.
