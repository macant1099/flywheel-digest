## Overview

Markable is an AI-driven creator commerce platform headquartered in Seattle, WA, founded by Joy Tang. The platform positions itself as the preferred tool for top-tier social media creators looking to systematically convert their influence into affiliate income. As of April 2026, Markable has powered over **$1 billion in sales** for more than **1,000 of the world's top social media creators**, placing it alongside LTK and ShopMy in the elite tier of creator-affiliate technology.

The platform's core thesis is straightforward but powerful: most creators leave enormous commission revenue on the table because their affiliate workflows are manual, slow, and fragmented. Markable plugs the leaks — automating DMs, generating deep links instantly, surfacing trending products, and creating shoppable visuals — so creators can monetize every post without spending hours managing links and follow-ups.

On **April 23, 2026**, Markable announced the launch of a **free creator tier**, opening its previously curated-only toolset to all creators. This marks a significant strategic shift from an exclusive top-creator focus to a broad creator economy play, targeting the estimated 200 million people (including 65% of Gen Z) who identify as creators worldwide.

Markable is designed to integrate with Amazon, major retailer affiliate programs, and social platforms including Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube, making it relevant to any Flywheel vendor running an affiliate or creator program.

## How It Works

Markable operates as a SaaS layer on top of existing affiliate programs and social platforms:

1. **Creator Onboarding**: Creator connects their social accounts and affiliate network accounts (Amazon, brand programs, etc.) to the Markable dashboard.
2. **Deep Link Generation**: Markable generates smart, platform-native deep links that route followers directly into native shopping apps (Amazon, brand storefronts), minimizing friction and cart abandonment.
3. **AutoDM Automation**: When creators post content, they set keyword triggers. Any follower who comments with the trigger word receives an automated DM with the relevant product link — converting comment engagement into sales without creator intervention.
4. **AI Product Collage**: Creators use AI to generate shoppable product collage images in seconds, turning standard posts into visually compelling commerce content.
5. **Viral Product Discovery**: Markable surfaces real-time trending products by category, allowing creators to stay ahead of purchasing trends and align content with high-converting products.
6. **Analytics & Optimization**: Creators track clicks, conversions, and commissions across all links and campaigns in a unified dashboard.

## Key Products & Services

### Smart Deep Links (Free Tier)
Route followers directly into native shopping apps for seamless, friction-free checkout. Unlimited links with no restrictions — every product in every post can be tagged.

### AutoDM (Free Tier — up to 2,000 replies)
Automatically triggers replies to comments containing specific keywords. Also enables follow-up DMs to anyone who commented in the past 7 days. Converts passive engagement into active purchase intent with zero creator time.

### AI Product Collage
AI-generated shoppable product collage images, built in seconds. Turns standard Instagram or TikTok posts into commerce-optimized visual content that drives clicks and conversions.

### Viral Products (Trend Intelligence)
Real-time surfacing of top-selling products by category. Creators see what's trending before their audiences do, enabling timely, high-converting content creation.

### Creator Analytics Dashboard
Unified commission and performance tracking across all affiliate links and social channels.

### Brand Partnership Marketplace (Paid)
For established creators on paid tiers, Markable facilitates direct brand deal negotiations and sponsored product placements alongside organic affiliate revenue.

## Pricing & Commission Model

Markable operates a freemium SaaS model:

- **Free Tier** (launched April 23, 2026): Smart deep links (unlimited), AutoDM (up to 2,000 replies/month), AI Product Collage, Viral Products discovery.
- **Paid Tiers**: Higher AutoDM volumes, advanced analytics, brand partnership access, priority creator support. Pricing not publicly disclosed; enrollment via markable.ai.
- **Revenue Model**: Markable earns through SaaS subscriptions and potentially revenue-share on brand partnerships facilitated through the platform.
- **Creator Earnings**: Creators keep their affiliate commissions from connected networks (Amazon, brand programs). Markable does not take a cut of affiliate commissions — it charges for the tooling.

## Strengths

- **Proven Scale**: $1B+ in creator-driven GMV before the free-tier launch is exceptional validation. This is not a startup promise — it's demonstrated results.
- **AI-First Workflow**: The AutoDM + deep link + AI collage combination addresses the most painful parts of the creator-affiliate workflow. Time savings of 2+ hours/day are a genuine productivity unlock.
- **Free Tier Timing**: Launching a free tier as social commerce is projected to exceed $100B in the US in 2026 is smart positioning. Markable captures the long tail of creators before Amazon, Meta, and others do.
- **Platform Agnosticism**: Works across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube — not locked into any single social ecosystem.
- **Top Creator Trust**: Starting with 1,000+ top creators and then opening to all gives Markable a credibility halo that new entrants can't replicate.
- **Vendor Exposure**: Brands whose products go viral on Markable's Viral Products feed get passive discovery by thousands of creators simultaneously.

## Weaknesses

- **Narrow Creator Focus**: Markable is optimized for social commerce creators, not traditional content publishers (blogs, newsletters, review sites). Its tools don't translate well to non-social affiliate traffic.
- **Amazon-Centric DNA**: Deep link routing appears heavily oriented toward Amazon and major retailer apps. Mid-market vendors without Amazon presence may have limited Markable integration options.
- **Competition Intensity**: LTK ($200M+ raised, massive creator network), ShopMy (growing rapidly), and Amazon's own Creator program all compete directly for top creator attention. Markable must differentiate aggressively.
- **Free Tier Sustainability**: Opening free access to 200M potential creators is a massive infrastructure bet. Conversion to paid tiers will be key to unit economics.
- **Brand Control**: Vendors don't control which creators use Markable to promote their products. If a low-quality creator goes viral promoting a brand's product via Markable, the brand has no recourse.
