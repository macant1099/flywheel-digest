## Overview

LTK (formerly rewardStyle and LIKEtoKNOW.it) is the pioneering creator commerce platform that transformed influencer product recommendations into a trackable, commissionable revenue channel. Founded in 2011 in Dallas by Amber Venz Box and Baxter Box, LTK invented much of the infrastructure that creator-led affiliate commerce runs on today — and it continues to drive more attributable retail revenue than virtually any other creator platform in existence.

With $238M in annual revenue as of 2026 and driving more than $5 billion in affiliate-tracked retail sales annually, LTK is the category definition. The platform connects 200,000+ creators with 7,000+ brand partners across 100+ countries, spanning fashion, beauty, home, travel, and beyond. On Cyber Monday 2024, Adobe Analytics reported that creators and affiliates accounted for 20.3% of U.S. retail revenue — and LTK's ecosystem is a significant driver of that number.

The company has operated under three brand names: rewardStyle (the brand-facing affiliate network, founded 2011), LIKEtoKNOW.it (the creator and consumer-facing shopping app), and LTK (the unified brand name adopted in 2021). Despite the rebrand, the company remains privately held and based in Dallas.

## How It Works

### For Creators
1. **Apply and Get Approved**: LTK maintains a curated creator base — creators apply and are approved based on content quality and audience fit. This selective approach maintains platform quality and brand trust.
2. **Link Products**: Approved creators use LTK's tools to generate shoppable links from any of 7,000+ brand partners. Links work across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, blogs, email newsletters, and the LTK app.
3. **Publish to the LTK App**: Creators post curated content to the LTK app (a shoppable social feed with millions of monthly shoppers), generating both commission income and brand visibility.
4. **Earn Commissions**: Creators earn commissions when followers purchase through their LTK links. Commission rates vary by brand and product category.
5. **Brand Campaigns**: LTK's team runs managed campaign services, connecting creators with brand briefs for sponsored content at scale.

### For Brands
1. **Program Integration**: Brands integrate with LTK through the platform's technology stack, setting commission rates and product eligibility for the creator marketplace.
2. **Creator Discovery**: Brands access LTK's 200,000+ creator directory with audience analytics, engagement data, and historical sales performance to identify the right creator partners.
3. **Campaign Management**: LTK offers both self-serve and managed campaign services, including strategy, creator casting, content approval, and performance measurement.
4. **Attribution and Reporting**: LTK's tracking technology provides creator-level attribution across social platforms, enabling brands to measure ROI at the individual creator and content level.

## Key Products & Services

### LTK Creator App
A mobile-first creation and publishing tool that enables creators to build shoppable content, generate commission links, and post to their LTK profile and external social channels. The technology is engineered to maintain attribution within closed social ecosystems like Instagram and Snapchat.

### LTK Shopping App
A consumer-facing shoppable social feed with millions of monthly active shoppers who browse creator recommendations and purchase directly. The LTK app functions as a curated shopping destination in its own right, independent of social media platforms.

### LTK Brand Platform
The brand-facing dashboard enabling self-serve creator discovery, campaign creation, commission management, and performance analytics. Brands can recruit creators, set commission structures, and track sales attribution across the entire LTK creator network.

### LTK Managed Campaigns
A full-service campaign management offering where LTK's team handles strategy, creator casting, brief development, content approval, and performance reporting. Designed for enterprise brand partners running large-scale influencer affiliate programs.

### LTK Connect
A direct integration pathway for brands and retailers to connect their product catalog to the LTK network, enabling any of the 200,000+ creators to link their products and earn commissions.

## Pricing & Commission Model

LTK operates a network commission model:

- **Creator Commissions**: Brands set commission rates (typically 5–20% depending on category), and LTK takes a platform percentage from each transaction before remitting creator earnings.
- **Brand Platform Fees**: Brands pay platform access fees for the brand dashboard, creator discovery tools, and campaign management features. Enterprise pricing is custom.
- **Managed Services**: LTK's managed campaign services carry separate fees, typically a percentage of campaign spend or flat agency-style fees.
- **No Public Rate Card**: LTK does not publish standard pricing. Brand terms are negotiated directly with the LTK sales team.

## Strengths

- **Scale and Tenure**: 15 years of brand relationships, 200,000+ creators, and 7,000+ brand partners make LTK the deepest creator affiliate network in the industry.
- **$5B+ in Annual Sales**: The volume of attributable retail revenue LTK drives is the most credible proof point for creator commerce ROI. Brands can point to a $5B track record.
- **LTK Shopping App**: The consumer-facing app creates a shopping destination with genuine organic demand, not just referred social traffic. This is a defensible asset that pure social commerce platforms lack.
- **Cross-Platform Attribution**: LTK's link technology maintains attribution across Instagram, Snapchat, TikTok, and YouTube — critical for accurate ROI measurement in closed social ecosystems.
- **Managed Services**: Full-service campaign management is a significant differentiator for large brands that need turnkey influencer affiliate programs.
- **International Reach**: 100+ countries and a global creator base give international brands access to localized creator partnerships at scale.

## Weaknesses

- **Selective Creator Approval**: LTK's curated approach means many creators are rejected or wait extended periods for approval. This limits the platform's growth speed versus open platforms like ShopMy.
- **Premium Pricing**: LTK's enterprise tiers and managed services carry significant cost. Mid-market brands may find the platform expensive relative to alternatives.
- **Competitor Pressure from ShopMy**: ShopMy's 196% growth in 2025 signals that younger creators increasingly prefer a more open, creator-friendly alternative. LTK may face talent migration if it doesn't evolve its creator experience.
- **Fashion/Lifestyle Concentration**: Like ShopMy, LTK's creator base and brand partnerships skew heavily toward fashion, beauty, and lifestyle. Technology, B2B, and SaaS brands have limited relevance.
- **Private Company Opacity**: LTK is privately held and shares limited operational data. Brand partners and investors have limited visibility into platform health and strategy.

## Market Position & Competitive Context

LTK is the established leader in creator affiliate commerce, with the deepest catalog of brands, creators, and a decade of attribution data:

- **LTK vs. ShopMy**: ShopMy is the fastest-growing challenger, with a more open creator onboarding model and 196% YoY growth. ShopMy competes directly for creators who find LTK's approval process too selective.
- **LTK vs. Amazon Associates / Levanta**: Amazon's ecosystem is Amazon-only. LTK is multi-brand and multi-retailer, enabling creators to build richer storefronts across categories.
- **LTK vs. Impact.com / Partnerize**: These are enterprise partnership management platforms. LTK is a creator-first marketplace. Brands often use both: a platform for program management and LTK for creator-specific distribution.
- **LTK vs. Traditional Networks (CJ, Awin)**: CJ and Awin have massive publisher networks but are not creator-native. LTK's creator-centric UX and the LTK Shopping app create a distinct consumer shopping behavior that traditional networks cannot replicate.

## Ideal Use Cases

LTK is best suited for:

- **Fashion, Beauty, Home, and Lifestyle Brands**: These are LTK's dominant categories with the deepest creator talent and the highest consumer intent.
- **Mid-to-Enterprise Brands ($50M+ revenue)**: LTK's pricing, managed services, and program scale are designed for brands with the budget to invest in premium creator partnerships.
- **Brands Running Instagram/TikTok-Heavy Strategies**: LTK's attribution technology is specifically engineered for closed social ecosystems, solving the biggest measurement challenge for social-first brands.
- **International Brands**: LTK's 100+ country presence and global creator base make it one of the few platforms capable of supporting a coordinated international creator affiliate strategy.
- **Brands Prioritizing GMV Over Cost Efficiency**: LTK drives volume. For brands where topline attributable sales matter more than cost-per-acquisition, LTK's scale is unmatched.
