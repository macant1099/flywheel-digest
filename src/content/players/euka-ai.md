## Overview

Euka AI is an AI-powered affiliate marketing platform built exclusively for the TikTok Shop ecosystem. Launched officially in January 2026, Euka positions itself as the first end-to-end solution for brands and agencies managing TikTok Shop affiliate and creator campaigns at scale. The platform addresses the core operational challenge of TikTok Shop affiliate marketing: manually managing thousands of creator relationships is time-consuming and leaves revenue on the table.

Euka was listed by Business Insider in March 2026 as one of 17 creator economy startups to watch, citing TikTok Shop's rapid growth in U.S. e-commerce as the key market driver. The platform has already facilitated campaigns for 2,000+ brands and partnerships with 200K+ verified TikTok Shop creators.

The company is officially listed in TikTok's App Store for sellers, signaling platform-level recognition as a first-party recommended solution.

## How It Works

Euka automates the full TikTok Shop affiliate creator lifecycle:

1. **AI Creator Discovery**: Search across 3.5M+ indexed TikTok Shop creators using visual, audio, and bio-based AI search with filtering by GMV, product category, follower count, and performance metrics.
2. **Automated Outreach**: Euka can send up to 1,000 direct messages and 2,500 targeted collaboration invites per day on behalf of the brand, dramatically scaling what would otherwise require a full-time team.
3. **Creator Relationship Management (CRM)**: Centralized system to track, nurture, and re-engage creators — turning one-time content collaborations into recurring revenue partnerships.
4. **Spark Code & Usage Rights Collection**: Automates the collection of Spark Codes for GMV MAX advertising campaigns and content usage rights for cross-platform deployment.
5. **Content Moderation**: AI-powered video screening against brand guidelines, with automatic flagging of non-compliant content for human review.
6. **Social Intelligence**: Competitive tracking of creator GMV performance, top-selling SKUs, viral video formulas, and high-converting content formats.
7. **Brand Contest Management**: Automated GMV and posting challenges with leaderboards, submission tracking, and payout management.
8. **Creator Marketplace**: Direct access to 10K+ vetted, trained TikTok Shop creators with campaign management and creator-proposed pricing.

### AI Creator Search

### Creator Outreach Automation
Automated TikTok DM campaigns, collaboration invites, and email sequences with AI-personalized messaging. Sends up to 1,000 DMs and 2,500 targeted invites per day — the equivalent of a large outreach team, automated.

### Creator CRM
Centralized relationship management dashboard tracking all creator partnerships, content performance, communication history, and re-engagement scheduling. Core to transforming one-time creators into recurring revenue engines.

### Social Intelligence Dashboard
Competitive intelligence on creator GMV, top-selling products, and viral content patterns. Enables brands to understand what's working across the category and reverse-engineer successful creator campaigns.

### Brand Contest Management
Automated challenge and contest management with leaderboards, submission tracking, and creator payouts. Drives competitive creator engagement and high-volume content output.

### Vetted Creator Marketplace
Direct access to 10K+ pre-vetted, trained TikTok Shop creators with known performance histories. Creators in the marketplace understand how TikTok Shop affiliate programs work, reducing onboarding friction.

## Pricing & Commission Model

Euka operates on a SaaS subscription model:

- **Subscription-Based**: Monthly or annual plans tiered by feature access and usage volume (DM limits, creator seats, marketplace access).
- **No Commission Override**: Euka does not take a percentage of affiliate sales. The platform fee is separate from creator commissions.
- **Creator Marketplace Fees**: The vetted marketplace may include placement fees or creator-proposed pricing for specific collaborations.
- **Pricing Transparency**: Full pricing not publicly listed; brands sign up and access pricing within the platform dashboard.

## Weaknesses

- **TikTok Platform Risk**: Euka's entire value proposition depends on TikTok Shop's continued presence and growth in the U.S. market. Platform policy changes, regulatory bans, or algorithm shifts could significantly impact the business.
- **Single Platform Focus**: Unlike multi-network platforms, Euka's value is almost entirely tied to TikTok Shop. Brands seeking cross-platform affiliate management need additional tools.
- **Early Stage Company**: Limited public information on funding, team size, and long-term roadmap. Company is still very young (2025/2026).
- **Outreach Automation Risks**: High-volume automated DMing can trigger TikTok platform restrictions if not carefully managed. The "1,000 DMs per day" ceiling reflects these limitations.
- **Creator Quality Variance**: Even with 3.5M+ indexed creators, affiliate quality on TikTok Shop varies widely. GMV-based filtering helps but does not guarantee brand-fit creators.
