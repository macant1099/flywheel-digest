## Overview

Connexity (formerly Shopzilla) is the largest independent retail marketing platform on the open web, headquartered in Los Angeles, CA. Founded in 1997, the company has evolved from a comparison shopping engine (Shopzilla) into a full-spectrum retail performance marketing platform serving both retailers (advertisers) and publishers (monetization partners).

Connexity operates as the connective tissue between retailer product catalogs and publisher content monetization. On the advertiser side, Connexity drives customer acquisition for retailers by placing their product listings across a massive publisher network. On the publisher side, Connexity is one of the highest-paying monetization platforms for commerce-oriented content, offering both CPC (cost-per-click) and CPA (cost-per-acquisition) compensation models across nearly **1 billion product offers** from the **IR 500's top merchants**.

In 2020, Connexity acquired **Skimlinks**, one of the largest independent affiliate networks for publishers, creating the largest independent customer acquisition channel in eCommerce. The combination of Connexity's CPC retail search budgets with Skimlinks' broad CPA affiliate coverage created a uniquely powerful publisher monetization stack. As of 2026, both Connexity and Skimlinks operate as part of the same corporate entity but maintain separate publisher-facing brands and product surfaces.

In 2021, Taboola acquired Connexity, integrating Connexity's retail marketing capabilities into Taboola's native advertising and content recommendation network. This acquisition gave Connexity access to Taboola's massive publisher network and native ad distribution while giving Taboola a high-intent, product-purchase-oriented commerce layer.

## How It Works

Connexity operates a two-sided marketplace connecting retailers and publishers:

**For Retailers (Advertisers):**
1. **Product Feed Submission**: Retailers submit product catalog feeds to Connexity, including pricing, availability, images, and descriptions.
2. **Campaign Management**: Connexity dynamically manages retailer campaigns across its publisher network, using CPC bidding and performance data to optimize spend.
3. **Customer Acquisition**: Connexity distributes retailer product listings across its publisher network, driving high-intent shoppers directly to product pages.
4. **CSS (Comparison Shopping Service)**: In the EU/UK, Connexity operates as a premium CSS provider, appearing in Google Shopping results under alternative CSS labels to help retailers diversify away from Google Shopping PLA dependence.

**For Publishers (Monetization Partners):**
1. **Integration**: Publishers integrate Connexity's tools (scripts, APIs, plugins) to automatically monetize product-related content.
2. **Product Key Solution**: Advanced toolset for building monetized commerce content at scale — including in-site product search, shopping widgets, automated link creation, and structured product data feeds.
3. **Dynamic Pricing**: Connexity uses dynamic pricing to automatically pay publishers based on the actual performance value of their traffic — above-average converting traffic earns more.
4. **Dual Monetization (CPC + CPA)**: Publishers can earn via CPC (each qualified click to a retailer earns a fee) and/or CPA (commission on actual sales), offering flexibility and revenue optimization.

## Key Products & Services

### Connexity Publisher Program
The core publisher monetization offering. Access to nearly 1 billion product offers, CPC and CPA monetization, automated earnings based on traffic quality, and Net-45 payment terms. Designed for publishers running shopping content at scale — review sites, comparison engines, lifestyle media, and commerce-oriented editorial.

### Product Key Solution
A suite of advanced publisher tools for building and scaling monetized commerce content:
- Scripts and plugins for automated link creation and maintenance
- Tools for building on-site and in-app product search and monetization components
- Structured product data access for building rich shopping experiences
- Integration with major CMS platforms and editorial workflows

### CSS (Comparison Shopping Service) — EU/UK
Connexity operates as a premium CSS provider in the European market, helping retailers appear in Google Shopping results without paying Google Shopping PLA rates. The CSS approach allows retailers to lower effective CPC costs while Connexity manages the underlying product campaign optimization.

### Skimlinks (Acquired 2020)
Connexity's acquired affiliate network for publisher content monetization. Skimlinks operates separately but synergistically — providing the CPA affiliate network layer that complements Connexity's CPC product placement network. Together they cover the full publisher monetization spectrum from click-based to sale-based compensation.

### Taboola Integration (Post-2021 Acquisition)
Following Taboola's acquisition of Connexity, Connexity's retail marketing capabilities have been integrated into Taboola's native advertising platform, extending Connexity's reach to Taboola's publisher network and native ad formats.

## Pricing & Commission Model

**Publisher Side:**
- **CPC Model**: Publishers earn a fee per qualified click to a retailer's product page. Rates are dynamically priced based on traffic quality and conversion performance.
- **CPA Model**: Publishers earn a percentage of sales generated through Connexity affiliate links. Commission rates vary by retailer and category.
- **Dynamic Earnings**: Connexity's platform automatically prices publisher earnings based on actual value delivered, rewarding high-converting traffic with higher payouts.
- **Payment Terms**: Net-45 (faster than many affiliate networks that operate on 60–90 day terms).

**Retailer Side:**
- **CPC Bidding**: Retailers bid for placement within Connexity's publisher network on a cost-per-click basis.
- **Performance Fees**: Some retailer arrangements include performance-based fees tied to actual sales outcomes.
- **CSS Fees**: EU/UK CSS services have separate pricing structures.

## Strengths

- **Scale**: 1 billion product offers across 500+ categories and full IR 500 merchant coverage gives publishers access to essentially any product in any retail category.
- **Largest Independent Platform**: Self-described (and widely cited) as the largest independent retail marketing platform on the open web — providing scale without the platform risk of Amazon or Google dependency.
- **Dual Monetization**: CPC + CPA flexibility allows publishers to optimize earnings based on their traffic type, with Connexity's dynamic pricing rewarding high-converting audiences.
- **Skimlinks Synergy**: The Connexity + Skimlinks combination creates a uniquely comprehensive publisher monetization stack, covering both the product placement (CPC) and affiliate (CPA) layers simultaneously.
- **CSS Leadership**: Connexity is a leading CSS provider in Europe, a strategically important position as retailers seek Google Shopping diversification.
- **Taboola Distribution**: The 2021 Taboola acquisition gives Connexity access to Taboola's native ad publisher network, extending reach beyond traditional affiliate publishers.

## Weaknesses

- **Less Visible Brand**: Connexity operates largely behind the scenes for end-users; publisher-facing brand recognition is weaker than Skimlinks, CJ, or Awin.
- **Not Creator-Focused**: Connexity is optimized for large-scale editorial publishers and product review sites rather than social creator monetization.
- **Enterprise-Centric**: The platform's tooling and pricing skew toward large-scale publisher operations; smaller creators or content sites may find better options in creator-focused platforms.
- **Taboola Acquisition Complexity**: Being part of Taboola (a publicly traded company focused on native advertising) creates potential strategic tension between Connexity's affiliate/performance identity and Taboola's native ad business.
- **Limited Direct Brand Visibility**: Retailers may not immediately recognize Connexity as distinct from their agency or network relationships, creating sales complexity.

## Recent Developments

- **CSS Strategy Evolution (March 2026)**: Connexity published analysis positioning its CSS offering as transitioning from "affiliate arbitrage" to "strategic media partner" — a signal that the company is repositioning its EU/UK business to capture retail media budgets beyond pure performance advertising.
- **Taboola Integration (Ongoing)**: Continued integration of Connexity's retail marketing capabilities into Taboola's native advertising platform and publisher network. The full synergy realization between these businesses remains a work-in-progress several years post-acquisition.
- **Skimlinks Product Key Evolution**: Ongoing development of Product Key tooling for publishers, with more recent investment in structured data access and in-app monetization components.
- **Skimlinks Acquisition (2020)**: Created the largest independent customer acquisition channel in eCommerce by combining Connexity's CPC shopping network with Skimlinks' broad CPA affiliate publisher network.
- **Taboola Acquisition (2021)**: Connexity was acquired by Taboola, integrating retail marketing into native advertising at scale.
