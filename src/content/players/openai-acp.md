## Overview

The Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP) is an open standard developed by OpenAI that serves as the connective layer between merchants and ChatGPT users, enabling AI-powered product discovery and purchasing directly within the ChatGPT interface. Launched in September 2025 with Etsy as the first merchant partner, ACP represents OpenAI's ambitious entry into the commerce ecosystem.

OpenAI was founded in 2015 by Sam Altman, Elon Musk, Greg Brockman, and others, and is headquartered in San Francisco. The company operates ChatGPT, the most widely used AI assistant, and in April 2026 closed a $122 billion funding round at a post-money valuation of $852 billion. Under the leadership of co-CEOs Sam Altman and Fidji Simo, OpenAI has been aggressively expanding ChatGPT from a conversational AI tool into a commerce platform.

ACP enables ChatGPT to act as a digital personal shopper: users describe what they want, ChatGPT recommends products, and purchases can be completed within the chat interface. The protocol is built with Stripe for payment processing and has expanded to include PayPal as an additional payment partner.

## How It Works

The Agentic Commerce Protocol creates a structured interaction between merchants and ChatGPT:

1. **Product Discovery**: Users ask ChatGPT shopping-related questions. ChatGPT uses its AI models to recommend relevant products from ACP-connected merchants, presenting visual browsing, comparison tables, and budget filtering.
2. **Merchant Connection**: ACP-connected merchants provide product catalog data, pricing, availability, and fulfillment information to ChatGPT through the protocol.
3. **Checkout (Evolving)**: Initially, OpenAI built Instant Checkout, enabling in-chat purchasing (first with Etsy, then expanding to Shopify merchants). In March 2026, OpenAI acknowledged the initial checkout "did not offer the level of flexibility" they aspired to and shifted focus to product discovery with redirect to merchant sites for checkout.
4. **Order Handling**: Orders, payments, and fulfillment are handled by the merchant using their existing systems. ChatGPT acts as the user's agent, securely passing information between user and merchant.
5. **Operator Agent**: OpenAI's Operator AI agent, introduced in January 2025, can perform web-based tasks on behalf of users, including shopping on sites like eBay, Etsy, and Instacart.

## Key Products & Services

### Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP)
The open standard for merchant-to-ChatGPT integration, handling product data exchange, user intent matching, checkout flow, and order management.

### ChatGPT Instant Checkout
In-chat purchasing powered by Stripe and PayPal, enabling users to buy products without leaving ChatGPT (currently being refined based on initial learnings).

### Product Discovery in ChatGPT
Visual browsing, image-based search, comparison tables, and budget filtering within the ChatGPT interface, with redirect to merchant sites for checkout.

### Operator
An autonomous AI agent that can navigate websites, fill forms, and complete transactions on behalf of users. Partnered with eBay, Etsy, Instacart, and other major platforms.

### Developer API
Tools for merchants and developers to integrate with ACP, manage product data, and participate in ChatGPT commerce.

## Pricing & Commission Model

OpenAI's commerce model charges merchants:

- **Transaction Fee**: 4% fee on transactions processed through ACP, significantly higher than Perplexity's zero-fee approach.
- **Stripe/PayPal Processing**: Standard payment processing fees apply in addition to the ACP transaction fee.
- **No Listing Fees**: No upfront costs to join the ACP merchant program.
- **Shopify Integration**: For Shopify merchants, integration is streamlined through Shopify's existing infrastructure.

## Strengths

- **Massive User Base**: ChatGPT has hundreds of millions of users, providing unmatched potential reach for merchant products.
- **AI-Native Commerce**: ChatGPT understands user intent in ways traditional search cannot, enabling highly personalized product recommendations.
- **Open Standard**: ACP is designed as an open protocol, encouraging broad adoption and ecosystem development.
- **Shopify Partnership**: Integration with Shopify's 1M+ merchants provides immediate scale for the ACP ecosystem.
- **Operator Agent**: The autonomous agent can handle complex, multi-step shopping tasks that go beyond simple search and recommend.
- **Payment Infrastructure**: Stripe and PayPal partnerships provide trusted, global payment processing.
- **$852B Valuation**: OpenAI's massive resources ensure continued investment in commerce features.

## Weaknesses

- **4% Transaction Fee**: Significantly higher than Perplexity's zero-fee model and competitive with some affiliate network overrides, creating merchant resistance.
- **Checkout Pivot**: The March 2026 acknowledgment that Instant Checkout "did not offer the level of flexibility" needed suggests the commerce experience is still being figured out.
- **Trust Concerns**: Users may be hesitant to make purchases directly through an AI chatbot, particularly for high-value items.
- **Competitive Protocols**: Google's Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP), announced January 2026, creates a competing standard. Merchants may need to support both.
- **Attribution Complexity**: As ChatGPT influences but doesn't always complete purchases (redirecting to merchant sites), attribution becomes complicated.
- **Early Stage**: ACP launched in September 2025, so the protocol is less than a year old with limited transaction volume data.

## Recent Developments

- **Checkout Strategy Pivot (March 2026)**: OpenAI shifted from in-chat Instant Checkout to a product discovery model with redirect to merchant sites, acknowledging that the initial checkout approach lacked flexibility.
- **Google UCP Competition (January 2026)**: Google announced the Universal Commerce Protocol as a competing standard for AI commerce, coming to Google Search AI Mode and Gemini.
- **PayPal Integration (October 2025)**: Added PayPal alongside Stripe as a payment option for ACP transactions.
- **Etsy Launch (September 2025)**: Launched Instant Checkout with Etsy as the first ACP merchant partner.
- **Shopify Expansion**: Over 1M Shopify merchants (including Glossier, SKIMS, Spanx, Vuori) being onboarded to ACP.
- **$122B Funding Round (April 2026)**: Massive funding round provides resources for continued commerce investment.

## Vendor Takeaway

OpenAI's Agentic Commerce Protocol is arguably the most consequential development in the affiliate and commerce ecosystem since the invention of the affiliate link itself. ChatGPT's user base, OpenAI's resources, and the ACP's open standard approach mean that AI-powered shopping will become a significant commerce channel. For Flywheel vendors, the key considerations are: (1) integrate with ACP as soon as possible, since early adopters will benefit from preferential placement as the ecosystem grows; (2) evaluate the 4% transaction fee against expected revenue, noting that it is higher than Perplexity's zero-fee model but lower than some marketplace commissions; (3) prepare for a dual-protocol world where both ACP and Google's UCP need to be supported; (4) ensure product data, pricing, and availability are accurate and machine-readable, as AI agents depend on structured data for recommendations; (5) monitor the evolving checkout model, as OpenAI is still refining how purchases are completed. ACP represents the transition from passive affiliate marketing to active agentic commerce, and vendors that prepare now will have a structural advantage.
