## Overview

ShopMy is a creator-first affiliate commerce platform that enables influencers and content creators to build personalized, shoppable digital storefronts and earn commissions on products they recommend. Founded in 2020 and headquartered in New York, ShopMy has emerged as one of the fastest-growing creator commerce platforms in the affiliate space, posting 196% year-over-year revenue growth from $27M in 2024 to an estimated $80M in 2025.

The platform has raised $166M in total funding across four rounds (most recently an $18.5M Series A in early 2025), backed by investors who see creator-led affiliate commerce as a category-defining growth market. With 150,000+ active creators and 1,000+ brand partners, ShopMy sits at the intersection of influencer marketing and performance-based affiliate commerce — a hybrid model that both sides of the market are increasingly demanding.

ShopMy is designed explicitly to remove friction for creators: no manual affiliate program applications, no per-network onboarding loops, and no complex tracking setup. Creators sign up, build their shop, and start linking products from any of the platform's integrated retailers. Commissions range from 10–30% on driven sales, with additional income available through paid brand partnership "Opportunities."

## How It Works

### For Creators
1. **Build a Shop**: Creators curate a personalized digital storefront ("ShopMy page") containing product links across multiple categories and retailers.
2. **Share Links**: ShopMy links are shareable across any platform — Instagram bio, TikTok, YouTube descriptions, newsletters, blogs. The platform is designed to work even within closed social platforms like Instagram where affiliate link tracking is historically difficult.
3. **Earn Commissions**: When a follower clicks through and purchases, the creator earns 10–30% commission. ShopMy consolidates attribution and payouts across all linked brands.
4. **Access Opportunities**: Brands post paid partnership briefs through ShopMy's Opportunities feature, allowing creators to apply for sponsored content deals in addition to organic affiliate earnings.

### For Brands
1. **List Products**: Brands integrate their product catalog and set commission rates on ShopMy's marketplace.
2. **Recruit Creators**: Brands can search the ShopMy creator directory to identify and invite relevant influencers, or post Opportunities for inbound applications.
3. **Track Performance**: ShopMy provides performance dashboards showing clicks, conversions, revenue driven, and creator-level attribution.
4. **Commission at Scale**: The platform handles payment processing and reporting across all creator relationships — one integration, many creator partners.

## Key Products & Services

### ShopMy Storefronts
Personalized shoppable pages where creators curate product recommendations across multiple brands and categories. Each storefront is a browsable, mobile-first experience that functions as both a portfolio and a revenue channel.

### Opportunities Marketplace
A curated marketplace where brands post paid partnership briefs and creators apply. Functions as a hybrid between an affiliate network and an influencer marketing platform, enabling both performance-based and flat-fee collaborations.

### Creator App & Link Tools
Mobile-first tools that enable creators to quickly add products to their storefront and generate affiliate links from any retailer. The technology is engineered to maintain attribution even within closed social platforms like Instagram and Snapchat.

### Brand Dashboard & Analytics
Performance reporting for brand partners including creator-level ROI, conversion rates, attributed revenue, and campaign analytics. Enables brands to identify top-performing creator partners and optimize commission structures.

### Multi-Network Integration
ShopMy aggregates affiliate relationships from multiple networks and direct retailer partnerships, eliminating the need for creators to manage individual network accounts across CJ, Awin, Rakuten, and others.

## Pricing & Commission Model

ShopMy operates a dual revenue model:

- **Creator Commissions**: Brands pay 10–30% commissions on sales driven by ShopMy creators. ShopMy takes a platform fee from the commission before passing the remainder to the creator.
- **Brand Subscription/Fees**: Brands pay for access to the creator marketplace and Opportunities feature. Specific pricing is not publicly disclosed; enterprise tiers are available for larger brand partners.
- **Paid Opportunities**: Brands pay flat fees for sponsored partnership briefs posted through the Opportunities marketplace. ShopMy takes a platform cut from these transactions.

The model allows brands to pay for performance (affiliate commissions) and reach (paid partnerships) through a single integrated relationship.

## Strengths

- **Explosive Growth**: 196% YoY revenue growth in 2025 signals genuine product-market fit in a competitive market.
- **Creator Experience**: Purpose-built for creators, with minimal friction to onboard and start earning. This drives creator adoption faster than traditional affiliate networks.
- **Hybrid Model**: The combination of performance commissions and paid partnership Opportunities creates multiple revenue streams for creators and multiple collaboration modes for brands.
- **Cross-Platform Link Intelligence**: ShopMy's link technology is engineered to maintain attribution in closed ecosystems like Instagram, solving a persistent pain point for creator-led affiliate programs.
- **Strong VC Backing**: $166M in total funding gives ShopMy runway to compete aggressively on product and creator acquisition.
- **Curated Network**: 1,000+ vetted brand partners and 150,000+ creators creates a quality marketplace versus the quantity-over-quality approach of open affiliate networks.

## Weaknesses

- **Not a Full SaaS Platform**: ShopMy is creator-oriented, not a robust program management tool. Brands needing advanced compliance, deep analytics, or complex commission logic should complement ShopMy with a platform like Impact or Everflow.
- **Fashion/Lifestyle Skew**: The platform's creator base skews heavily toward fashion, beauty, lifestyle, and home categories. Brands in B2B, SaaS, or financial services will find limited creator fit.
- **Commission Opacity**: ShopMy's take rate from the creator-brand commission split is not publicly disclosed, making total program cost comparison difficult for brands.
- **Brand Self-Service Limits**: Unlike Impact or CJ, brands have limited control over granular campaign configuration. The platform is designed for scale and simplicity, not customization.
- **Newer Platform**: Founded 2020, ShopMy lacks the decade-long publisher relationships and brand trust of LTK or CJ. Enterprise brand procurement teams may require more vetting.

## Market Position & Competitive Context

ShopMy occupies a distinct position as a creator-first affiliate platform focused on the mid-to-premium influencer segment (typically 10K–2M followers):

- **ShopMy vs. LTK**: LTK is the incumbent with ~$238M revenue and a decade of brand relationships. ShopMy is the challenger growing nearly 200% YoY and positioned as more creator-friendly and modern. Both compete directly for the same creator and brand relationships.
- **ShopMy vs. Amazon Associates / Levanta**: Amazon's affiliate ecosystem focuses on Amazon-only products. ShopMy is multi-retailer, enabling creators to build richer storefronts spanning many brands.
- **ShopMy vs. Traditional Networks (CJ, Awin)**: Traditional networks require creators to apply to each program individually. ShopMy aggregates brand access and simplifies the creator experience significantly.
- **ShopMy vs. Impact.com**: Impact is an enterprise partnership management platform. ShopMy is a consumer-facing creator marketplace. They serve different ends of the brand-creator spectrum and can coexist.

## Ideal Use Cases

ShopMy is well-suited for:

- **Fashion, Beauty, and Lifestyle Brands**: These verticals dominate ShopMy's creator base. High-intent, high-engagement audiences with strong purchase intent.
- **Direct-to-Consumer Brands**: DTC brands looking to scale creator partnerships without the overhead of managing individual influencer contracts.
- **Mid-Market Brands ($10M–$200M revenue)**: ShopMy's brand-facing tooling is optimized for brands that want creator scale without enterprise-level program management complexity.
- **Brands Prioritizing Instagram/TikTok ROI**: ShopMy's cross-platform attribution technology is specifically engineered for closed social ecosystems.
