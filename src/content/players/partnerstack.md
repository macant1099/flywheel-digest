## Overview

PartnerStack is the dominant affiliate and partner management platform in the B2B SaaS segment. While impact.com and CJ Affiliate serve mostly e-commerce and D2C brands, PartnerStack carved out a highly defensible niche by building specifically for software vendors — with a publisher marketplace populated by B2B content creators, review sites, technology bloggers, and business consultants who understand and actively promote software tools.

For LiveChannel vendors with a **software, subscription, or SaaS component**, PartnerStack is the most relevant platform because its publisher ecosystem matches the buyer audience (business owners, marketers, operators) rather than consumer shoppers.

## How It Works

PartnerStack operates a **two-sided platform** like a traditional network, but purpose-built for software partnerships:

1. **Vendors** (advertisers) set up their program with commission structures (% of MRR, flat-rate CPA per trial signup, etc.)
2. Programs appear in the **PartnerStack Marketplace** — a curated directory of B2B software programs that partners actively browse
3. **Partners** (publishers, consultants, reviewers, agencies) apply to programs
4. PartnerStack tracks referrals via links, handles automatic commission calculations (including recurring MRR commissions), and pays partners monthly
5. Vendors access dashboards to manage partner applications, view conversion funnels, and automate partner communications

The network effect here is strong: B2B review sites like G2, Capterra, and SaaS-focused content creators maintain PartnerStack accounts specifically because their target advertiser programs live there.

## Key Products & Services

**Partner Marketplace** — Curated directory of 80,000+ B2B-oriented partners actively looking for software programs to promote. Unlike general affiliate networks, these partners understand SaaS metrics and have B2B audiences.

**Recurring Commission Automation** — Native support for MRR-based commission structures. If a referred customer stays subscribed for 12 months, PartnerStack auto-calculates and pays the recurring monthly commission without manual reconciliation — a critical feature for SaaS.

**Partner Portal** — White-labeled partner portal that vendors embed in their own website. Partners log in to grab links, view earnings, access training materials, and submit payouts — all under the vendor's brand.

**Partner Tiers** — Multi-tier program management for vendors who want to differentiate between gold/silver/bronze partners with different commission rates and benefits.

**Reseller Management** — Beyond affiliates, PartnerStack supports reseller partner programs where partners actually sell the product on the vendor's behalf (not just refer traffic).

**Integration Ecosystem** — Connects with CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce), billing platforms (Stripe, Chargebee), and attribution tools.

## Pricing / Commission Model

PartnerStack pricing is SaaS-fee based with a transaction component:
- **Essentials**: ~$500/month (up to $5K/month in managed commissions)
- **Growth**: ~$1,000/month
- **Enterprise**: Custom

Additionally charges a **rev-share percentage** on managed payouts (typically 2–5%), though at higher tiers this is negotiated.

Partners use PartnerStack for free and receive commissions set by the advertiser.

## Strengths

- **B2B publisher quality**: The marketplace has actual B2B review writers, consultants, and integration partners — not coupon clippers and cashback apps
- **Recurring commission native**: Handles subscription/MRR commission logic that general networks handle badly or not at all
- **Reseller support**: The only major affiliate platform that also handles reseller partner channels in the same interface
- **Brand recognition in B2B**: Being on PartnerStack signals to B2B publishers that a program is worth joining — strong marketplace brand
- **White-label portal**: Vendors can present a fully branded partner experience without building custom infrastructure

## Weaknesses

- **B2B / SaaS focused**: If you're a pure DTC e-commerce or physical goods vendor, PartnerStack's publisher base isn't ideal — they don't review sneakers
- **Publisher volume**: 80K partners is smaller than CJ or Awin's millions, though quality compensates for quantity in B2B
- **Higher cost at scale**: As payout volumes grow, the rev-share component makes it more expensive than pure SaaS alternatives
- **Less geographic diversity**: Strongest in North America; EU and APAC B2B publisher depth thinner than U.S.

## Vendor Takeaway

PartnerStack is **essential for LiveChannel vendors with a software or subscription product** — or any vendor that sells to B2B buyers. The publisher ecosystem matches the audience, the commission tooling handles SaaS economics natively, and the marketplace brand drives inbound partner interest without heavy outreach.

**Best fit for:**
- SaaS tools, platforms, and apps targeting business users
- Subscription services where recurring commissions matter
- Vendors wanting reseller channels alongside affiliate programs

**Not ideal for:** Physical goods, pure DTC consumer products, or vendors where coupon/loyalty/cashback publishers drive meaningful volume.
