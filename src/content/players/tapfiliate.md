## Overview

Tapfiliate is a cloud-based affiliate and referral marketing platform purpose-built for growing SMBs and mid-market brands. Founded in 2014 and headquartered in Amsterdam, the company positions itself as the simplest, most affordable way for brands to launch and scale their own affiliate programs — without the overhead of a traditional network or the complexity of enterprise SaaS platforms like Impact.com or Partnerize.

The platform's defining competitive differentiator is its flat-rate, transparent pricing model: brands pay a fixed monthly fee and keep 100% of the value of commissions paid to affiliates. There are no network override fees, no percentage-of-revenue charges, and no hidden costs. This makes Tapfiliate especially attractive to ecommerce brands on Shopify, BigCommerce, or WooCommerce that are standing up their first affiliate program and want predictable costs.

Tapfiliate is a SaaS tool in the same category as Refersion and GoAffPro, but differentiates with cleaner UX, stronger MLM/multi-level marketing support, and broader integration breadth out of the box.

## How It Works

Tapfiliate follows a straightforward brand-owned affiliate program model:

1. **Setup & Integration**: Brands connect Tapfiliate to their ecommerce platform or payment processor via one of 30+ pre-built integrations (Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Stripe, PayPal, Wix, and more). A REST API enables custom integrations for non-standard stacks.
2. **Affiliate Recruitment & Onboarding**: Brands create a branded affiliate portal where publishers can sign up, access tracking links, banners, and promotional assets.
3. **Tracking**: Tapfiliate tracks clicks, conversions, and commissions in real time. Cookie-based tracking is standard; custom tracking methods available on higher tiers.
4. **Commission Management**: Flexible commission structures including flat-rate, percentage-based, recurring (subscription), coupon-based, item/category-based, and MLM/multi-level. Bonus systems for goal-based incentives.
5. **Payments**: Automated affiliate payouts via Trolley (formerly TransferWise for Business) integration on Scale and Enterprise plans.
6. **Reporting**: Real-time dashboard showing clicks, conversions, commission accrual, and affiliate performance.

## Key Products & Services

### Launch Plan ($89/month)
Entry-level tier. Supports 1 affiliate program, up to 50 affiliates, 5,000 clicks/month, and 500 conversions/month. Includes 30+ pre-built integrations, real-time reporting, recurring commissions, coupon tracking, and bulk actions. Overage fees apply ($1.50/1,000 clicks; $15/1,000 conversions).

### Scale Plan ($179/month)
Growth tier. Unlimited affiliates and affiliate programs, 100,000 clicks/month, 10,000 conversions/month, unlimited team members up to 5. Adds advanced affiliate management (groups, private assets, caps management, product XML feeds), advanced commission management (group commissions, item/category-based, bonus system), multi-level marketing (MLM) with advanced sales goals, automated payouts via Trolley, and deeplinks/shortlinks.

### Enterprise Plan (Custom)
High-volume tier. Tailored click and conversion limits, unlimited team members, detailed API click reports, unlimited triggered notifications, full white-label capability, and a dedicated personal manager. 30-day trial available.

### Integrations Layer
30+ native integrations covering major ecommerce platforms (Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, PrestaShop), payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, Paddle), and SaaS tools. REST API for custom builds. Zapier connectivity for workflow automation.

## Pricing & Commission Model

Tapfiliate uses a clean flat-rate SaaS model with no network override fees:

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) | Affiliates | Clicks/mo | Conversions/mo |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Launch | $89 | $74 | 50 | 5,000 | 500 |
| Scale | $179 | $149 | Unlimited | 100,000 | 10,000 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Unlimited | Tailored | Tailored |

Overage fees apply on Launch ($1.50/1,000 clicks; $15/1,000 conversions) and Scale ($1.00/1,000 clicks; $10/1,000 conversions). A 14-day free trial is available on Launch and Scale; Enterprise gets a 30-day trial upon request.

This model means brands pay only the platform fee — all commission payments go 100% to affiliates. Total cost of ownership at small-to-mid volume is typically lower than networks that charge 20-30% overrides on top of affiliate commissions.

## Strengths

- **Price Transparency**: Flat-rate pricing with no hidden fees is highly appealing to SMBs and growing ecommerce brands that want predictable affiliate program costs.
- **Easy Setup**: 30+ pre-built integrations mean brands on major platforms can go live in hours, not weeks.
- **MLM/Multi-Level Support**: Tapfiliate's multi-level marketing feature (with advanced sales goals on Scale) is relatively unique in the SMB-focused segment, enabling brands to build tiered ambassador or distributor networks.
- **Recurring Commissions**: Native support for subscription/recurring commissions is critical for SaaS brands — well-handled in Tapfiliate vs. some alternatives.
- **Full White-Label**: Enterprise plan white-labeling means brands can offer a fully branded affiliate experience with no Tapfiliate branding.
- **Clean UX**: Generally praised for ease of use and affiliate-facing portal quality compared to legacy tools.

## Weaknesses

- **Small Affiliate Limits on Entry Tier**: 50-affiliate cap on Launch is restrictive — brands with any meaningful program will hit this quickly and need to upgrade.
- **No Built-in Publisher Marketplace**: Unlike Impact.com or CJ, Tapfiliate does not have a network of publishers for discovery. Brands must recruit affiliates from scratch.
- **Click/Conversion Volume Caps**: Monthly limits on clicks and conversions (with overage fees) can make costs unpredictable for high-traffic programs at lower tiers.
- **Limited Analytics Depth**: Reporting is real-time and functional but less sophisticated than enterprise-grade platforms offering multi-touch attribution, cohort analysis, or predictive modeling.
- **No Fraud Detection**: Tapfiliate lacks built-in affiliate fraud detection tools — brands with large programs must rely on manual monitoring or third-party solutions.
- **SMB Focus Limits Enterprise Features**: Brands that outgrow the Scale plan may find Tapfiliate's Enterprise tier lacking compared to Impact.com or Partnerize for complex global programs.

## Recent Developments

## Competitive Context

Tapfiliate competes most directly with:

- **Refersion**: Similar SMB-focused model; Refersion has broader brand recognition in the US DTC/ecommerce space but comparable pricing. Tapfiliate edges Refersion on MLM support and integration breadth.
- **GoAffPro**: Lower-cost alternative (free tier available), but less feature-rich at scale. GoAffPro targets micro-brands; Tapfiliate targets growing brands with meaningful affiliate programs.
- **LeadDyno**: Older SMB affiliate platform; Tapfiliate generally rated higher on UX and feature modernity.
- **PartnerStack**: Focused on B2B SaaS rather than ecommerce — different primary use case.

At higher volumes, Tapfiliate starts to lose ground to Impact.com and Everflow, which offer superior tracking granularity, fraud protection, and publisher marketplace access.

## Vendor Takeaway

Tapfiliate is the right choice for LiveChannel vendors in the early-to-mid growth stage that want to launch a clean, professionally managed affiliate program without committing to enterprise platform costs. The flat-rate pricing, fast integration with Shopify and Stripe, and MLM support make it particularly useful for brands building structured creator ambassador programs. However, vendors should plan for platform migration as programs scale — Tapfiliate's volume caps and lack of publisher discovery mean larger programs will eventually outgrow it. For vendors already generating meaningful affiliate GMV (>$5M/year), starting on Impact.com or Everflow is likely more cost-effective long-term.
