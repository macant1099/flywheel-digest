## Overview

Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search engine that has rapidly expanded into commerce, creating one of the first fully integrated AI shopping experiences. Founded in August 2022 by Aravind Srinivas (CEO, formerly of OpenAI and Google DeepMind), Denis Yarats, Johnny Ho, and Andy Konwinski, the company is headquartered in San Francisco and has reached a valuation exceeding $21 billion following its Series E-6 funding round in early 2026. Total funding raised exceeds $1.72 billion.

Perplexity processes approximately 30 million queries daily, with over 20% month-over-month growth. The company launched its shopping features in late 2024 for Pro users in the US and has since expanded them significantly. The Perplexity Merchant Program is free for merchants (zero fees, zero commissions, zero listing charges), positioning Perplexity as a fundamentally different commerce channel compared to traditional affiliate networks or OpenAI's 4% transaction fee model.

In February 2026, Perplexity pivoted away from its advertising model, choosing to focus on enhancing the shopping experience and growing its Pro membership, signaling that commerce is central to its long-term business strategy.

## How It Works

Perplexity Shopping integrates product discovery and purchasing directly into the AI search experience:

1. **AI-Powered Product Discovery**: When users ask shopping-related questions ("What's the best espresso machine under $500?"), Perplexity provides AI-curated product recommendations with citations, pricing, and availability information.
2. **Merchant Program Data**: Merchants that join the free Merchant Program share product catalog data with Perplexity, ensuring accurate pricing, specs, and availability. This increases the likelihood of their products being recommended.
3. **Buy with Pro (In-Chat Checkout)**: Pro users can purchase select products directly within Perplexity using the "Buy with Pro" feature, powered by PayPal integration. No need to leave the Perplexity interface.
4. **Instant Buy (PayPal Integration)**: A new checkout feature built with PayPal as the payment method, enabling purchases from participating retailers without leaving Perplexity.
5. **Snap to Shop (Visual Search)**: Users can photograph a product with their smartphone camera and find similar products available for purchase, expanding discovery beyond text queries.

## Key Products & Services

### Perplexity Merchant Program
A free program for merchants to share product data with Perplexity, ensuring accurate product representation in AI search results. Benefits include increased recommendation likelihood, payment integration for Buy with Pro, and free API access for merchant-side Perplexity-powered search.

### Buy with Pro
In-chat checkout for Perplexity Pro users, enabling seamless purchases from select merchants without leaving the search interface.

### Instant Buy
PayPal-powered checkout available to users purchasing from participating merchants directly through Perplexity.

### Snap to Shop
Visual search feature enabling product discovery through smartphone camera, bridging offline product discovery with online purchasing.

### AI Product Recommendations
Curated, citation-backed product recommendations generated by Perplexity's AI models, drawing on merchant data, reviews, and expert sources.

## Pricing & Commission Model

Perplexity's commerce model is radically different from traditional affiliate marketing:

- **Zero Merchant Fees**: The Merchant Program charges no fees, commissions, or listing costs. This contrasts sharply with ChatGPT's 4% ACP transaction fee and traditional affiliate network overrides.
- **Pro Subscription**: Perplexity earns revenue through its Pro subscription ($20/month), which includes shopping features like Buy with Pro.
- **Future Monetization**: Perplexity may introduce commerce-related revenue models in the future, but the current zero-fee approach is designed for merchant acquisition and ecosystem growth.
- **No Advertising Model (as of 2026)**: Perplexity pivoted away from advertising, suggesting commerce and subscriptions are the primary revenue focus.

## Strengths

- **Zero-Fee Model**: No merchant fees, commissions, or listing charges make Perplexity the most merchant-friendly AI commerce platform.
- **AI-Native Shopping**: Product discovery is AI-native rather than adapted from traditional search, creating a fundamentally different and potentially better shopping experience.
- **Rapid Growth**: 30M+ daily queries with 20% month-over-month growth indicate massive user adoption.
- **PayPal Integration**: Partnership with PayPal for Instant Buy provides trusted, frictionless checkout.
- **Visual Search**: Snap to Shop creates unique discovery pathways beyond text queries.
- **$21B+ Valuation**: Strong funding ($1.72B raised) and valuation provide resources for continued investment in commerce features.
- **Citation-Based Trust**: AI recommendations include citations to sources, building user trust in product suggestions.

## Weaknesses

- **Pro-Gated Features**: The best shopping features (Buy with Pro) require a $20/month Pro subscription, limiting the commerce audience to paying users.
- **Nascent Commerce Platform**: Perplexity Shopping is still young and lacks the merchant coverage and checkout sophistication of established ecommerce platforms.
- **Uncertain Revenue Model**: The zero-fee merchant model is generous but may not be sustainable. Future monetization changes could disrupt merchant expectations.
- **Limited Merchant Count**: 5,000+ merchants is a fraction of what traditional affiliate networks offer.
- **User Intent Distribution**: Most Perplexity users search for information, not products. Converting search behavior to shopping behavior is an ongoing challenge.
- **Competitive Pressure**: ChatGPT Shopping, Google AI Shopping, and Amazon are all building competing AI commerce experiences.

## Recent Developments

- **Advertising Pivot (February 2026)**: Abandoned advertising model to focus exclusively on shopping experience and Pro membership growth.
- **PayPal Integration (2025)**: Selected PayPal to power Instant Buy and agentic commerce capabilities, enabling in-chat checkout.
- **5,000+ Merchant Milestone**: Expanded merchant program to over 5,000 participating merchants.
- **Snap to Shop**: Launched visual search for product discovery through smartphone camera.
- **Buy with Pro Expansion**: Continued expanding the in-chat checkout experience to more merchants and product categories.
