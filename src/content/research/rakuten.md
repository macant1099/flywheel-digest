# Rakuten Advertising — Network Profile

## Overview

Rakuten Advertising is the affiliate division of Rakuten Group ($16B revenue). Traces origins to LinkShare (1996). Operates as a curated, managed network with fewer merchants but focuses on enterprise-level partnerships. Unique advantage: Rakuten Rewards (20M+ US members).

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Merchants | ~1,000 curated |
| Publishers | 150,000+ |
| Rakuten Rewards Members | 20M+ US |
| Parent | Rakuten Group ($16B revenue) |
| Founded | 1996 (as LinkShare) |

## Vendor Relevance

**Anker:** Rakuten Rewards (20M+ cashback members) represents a unique distribution opportunity. CE vertical rates are 2-6%, making Anker's 8% competitive.

**Priority Bicycles:** Not an ideal fit due to Rakuten's enterprise focus and lack of cycling-endemic publishers.

## Strengths

- Premium brand portfolio (Walmart, Macy's, Nordstrom, Samsung)
- Rakuten Rewards ecosystem with 20M+ purchase-intent members
- Weekly payments (4x/month, most frequent of any major network)
- Innovation Labs (2026) for AI-driven optimization
