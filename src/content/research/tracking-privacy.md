Monitoring cookie deprecation, server-side tracking, and attribution shifts affecting affiliate programs.

Multiple industry sources confirm 2026 is the inflection point where third-party cookie deprecation effects are materially impacting affiliate attribution accuracy. Google's gradual rollout (rather than hard cutoff) delayed the impact, but privacy-first browser behavior across Safari and Firefox has created real gaps. Networks and vendors not running server-side tracking risk losing ~15% of revenue to attribution errors. First-party data strategies are now table-stakes, not aspirational.
