# CJ Affiliate — Network Profile

## Overview

CJ Affiliate (formerly Commission Junction) is one of the oldest and largest affiliate networks, founded in 1998. Part of Publicis Groupe since 2019. Processes billions in annual commerce across virtually every major ecommerce vertical.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Brands | 3,800+ |
| Publishers | 167,000+ active |
| Marketplace Programs | 20,500+ |
| Parent Company | Publicis Groupe |
| Founded | 1998 |

## Vendor Relevance

**Anker:** Not on CJ, but CJ hosts competing CE brands (Samsung, HP, GoPro). Natural expansion candidate given CE vertical strength.

**Priority Bicycles:** Smaller cycling/outdoor presence than AvantLink, but CJ's scale could broaden publisher reach.

## Strengths

- Enterprise-grade technology with S2S tracking and cookieless attribution
- Epsilon identity graph (230M+ US consumer profiles) for cross-device tracking
- Premium merchant portfolio attracts high-quality publishers
- 28+ years of operational history and institutional credibility
