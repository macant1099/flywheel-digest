# ShareASale — Network Profile

## Overview

ShareASale was founded in 2000 and acquired by Awin in 2017. Platform officially closed October 6, 2025, with all accounts migrated to Awin. The consolidation reduced the "Big 5" US affiliate networks to a "Big 4."

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Merchants (Pre-Migration) | 16,550+ |
| Advertisers (Migrated) | 9,500+ |
| Publishers (Migrated) | 250,000+ |
| Platform Status | Closed October 6, 2025 |

## Vendor Relevance

**Anker:** Not historically on ShareASale. The consolidated Awin marketplace offers expanded competitive landscape.

**Priority Bicycles:** Competing cycling brands (ICAN Cycling, Velowave) migrated from ShareASale to Awin, potentially increasing competitive pressure.

## Legacy Significance

- ShareASale's transparency culture (EPC, conversion rates, reversal rates) set industry standards preserved by Awin
- The migration represents the most significant affiliate industry consolidation in recent years
- Industry reduced from "Big 5" to "Big 4" US networks
