# Impact.com — Network Profile

## Overview

Impact.com (formerly Impact Radius) is a partnership management platform founded in 2008 by founding CJ team members. Distinguished by its SaaS model — brands build and manage their own programs rather than relying on a managed network. ~$270M ARR with ~20% YoY growth.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| ARR | ~$270M |
| Partner-Referred GMV | ~$120 billion |
| Growth | ~20% YoY |
| Founded | 2008 |
| Employees | 1,000+ |

## Vendor Relevance

**Anker:** Impact's Amazon integration and DTC brand expertise make it a strong candidate if Anker adopts a partnership platform.

**Priority Bicycles:** Impact's broader publisher base could expand reach beyond AvantLink, but $2,500+/month pricing may be prohibitive.

## Strengths

- Most modern and flexible technology stack in affiliate industry
- Native Shopify and Amazon integrations
- Dynamic commissioning engine with rules-based rate adjustment
- Partnership breadth beyond traditional affiliate (influencer, B2B, mobile)
