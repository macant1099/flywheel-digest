# Bicycles & Outdoor Recreation — Vertical Affiliate Landscape

## Overview

The bicycles and outdoor recreation vertical is characterized by passionate, community-driven audiences, high average order values, and a publisher ecosystem dominated by enthusiast content creators. Global bicycle market projected at $34.1B by 2026 (3.8% CAGR).

## Market Economics

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Global Bicycle Market (2026) | $34.1 billion |
| US Bicycle Market | ~$8-9 billion |
| E-bike Growth | 15-20% CAGR |
| Avg Enthusiast Purchase | $500-$3,000 |
| Affiliate Channel Contribution | 3-7% of online bike sales |

## Commission Benchmarks

Complete bicycles: 3-8%, e-bikes: 5-10%, parts/components: 5-10%, accessories: 5-12%, cycling apparel: 7-15%.

## AvantLink Dominance

AvantLink is the defining network for this vertical, with REI, Patagonia, Trek, Priority Bicycles, Aventon, and Brompton among its merchants. Curated publisher base ensures brand-safe, content-quality placements.

## Emerging Trends

- E-bike explosion (15-20% CAGR) with aggressive affiliate recruitment
- AI shopping agents beginning to recommend bicycles
- Community commerce aligned with cycling culture
- Subscription/maintenance models emerging
