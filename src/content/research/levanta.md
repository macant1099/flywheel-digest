Amazon-native affiliate and creator platform. Manages affiliate programs for Amazon, Shopify, and Walmart sellers.

Levanta announced the acquisition of Perch+, one of the earliest affiliate networks built specifically for Amazon sellers and publishers. The deal brings Perch+'s network of sellers and affiliate partners directly into Levanta's platform. This continues a trend of consolidation in the Amazon affiliate ecosystem, reducing optionality for publishers but strengthening Levanta's position as the dominant Amazon-native affiliate platform alongside its existing Shopify and Walmart integrations.
