# Awin — Network Profile

## Overview

Awin (formerly Affiliate Window) is one of the world's largest affiliate marketing networks, connecting over 30,000 advertisers with 260,000+ active publishers across 180+ countries. Majority-owned by Axel Springer. In October 2025, Awin completed the full migration of all ShareASale accounts to its platform.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Advertisers | 25,000-30,000+ |
| Publishers | 260,000+ active |
| Countries | 180+ |
| Annual Sales | $18B+ for advertisers |
| ShareASale Integration | Completed October 2025 |

## Vendor Relevance

**Anker:** Not on Awin, but Awin's consolidated publisher base makes it a strong expansion candidate.

**Priority Bicycles:** On AvantLink, not Awin. Several competing cycling brands migrated from ShareASale to Awin.

## Strengths

- Unmatched global scale (30,000+ brands, 260,000+ publishers)
- ShareASale consolidation creates largest US affiliate network
- First-mover AI integration with platform assistant
- Strong attribution and cookieless tracking solutions
