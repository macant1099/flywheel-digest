# Consumer Electronics — Vertical Affiliate Landscape

## Overview

Consumer electronics is one of the largest and most competitive affiliate verticals, driven by high purchase intent and a content-rich ecosystem. Global market exceeded $1.15 trillion in 2025. Amazon accounts for an estimated 50-60% of all affiliate-driven CE purchases in the US.

## Market Economics

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Global Market (2025) | $1.15 trillion |
| US Ecommerce Share | ~40% online |
| Affiliate Contribution | 5-10% of online CE sales |
| Average Order Value | $75-$500 |

## Commission Benchmarks

Charging & power: 5-10%, audio & headphones: 3-8%, smart home: 3-8%, laptops: 1-5%. Amazon Associates pays only 1-4%, creating opportunity for brands with direct programs at 2-3x those rates.

## Anker's Position

Anker's 8% direct commission significantly exceeds the Amazon Associates rate (1-4%) and category average (3-5%). Strong brand recognition and wide product range create multiple purchase occasions for publishers.

## Emerging Trends

- AI-powered product recommendations increasingly driving CE affiliate traffic
- Creator-affiliate convergence (YouTube/TikTok creators using affiliate links)
- DTC shift as brands escape Amazon's margin compression
- Levanta enabling 15-25% brand-funded commissions on Amazon purchases
