# LiveChannel — Network Profile

## Overview

LiveChannel is an emerging commerce platform headquartered in Seattle that positions itself as "The 3rd Way" of ecommerce — a hybrid model combining DTC and marketplace into "Connected Commerce." Unlike traditional affiliate networks, LiveChannel creates community-powered commerce channels connecting brands directly with communities.

## Key Positioning

- Not a traditional affiliate network
- Not a pure marketplace
- Not a standard DTC platform
- A hybrid "connected commerce" model

## Vendor Relevance

**Anker:** Strong community followings in tech enthusiast, charging/power, and home audio communities are ideal for LiveChannel's model.

**Priority Bicycles:** Cycling communities (local bike clubs, commuter groups, urban cycling forums) are tight-knit and trust-driven — a natural fit.

## Strengths

- Novel connected commerce model addressing DTC and marketplace limitations
- Community-first approach leveraging trust and context
- Deterministic attribution (platform-mediated, no cookie dependency)
- Differentiated positioning with no direct competitors at the community + DTC + marketplace intersection
