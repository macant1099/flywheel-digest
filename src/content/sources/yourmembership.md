# YourMembership

## What they do
YourMembership is a mid-market association management system (AMS) owned by Community Brands (now Momentive Software). It handles the core back office of small-to-mid-sized professional associations: member database, dues billing, auto-renewal, event registration, email, a member directory, an online community, a basic CMS website, and the YM Careers job board. Community Brands reports 5,000+ customers across 32 countries, with YourMembership specifically positioned for associations with 2–15 staff and $500K–$5M operating budgets.

Zooming in on member-deals: there is no dedicated affinity/perks module in the product. "Member benefits" appears extensively in YourMembership's marketing and blog content as a *strategy* associations should run, but the AMS itself does not ship a structured deals marketplace.

## The member-deals capability
In practice, associations using YourMembership deliver affinity discounts through generic surfaces: (1) a "Member Benefits" or "Partners" page built in the CMS and gated to logged-in members, (2) announcements through the built-in email tool, and (3) posts inside the online community module. Partners are typically listed as logos with a short blurb, a link, and sometimes a code or a PDF. There is no transaction tracking, no commission ledger, no coupon infrastructure, no partner-facing portal, and no structured "offer" data type in the schema. YourMembership actively publishes thought-leadership content telling associations to *build* affinity programs for non-dues revenue — but the associations build them in spreadsheets and manual partner agreements, not in the software.

## Strengths (relative to LiveChannel's private-network play)
Members already authenticate in the YourMembership portal for dues, events, and directory lookups — so the identity + gated-audience substrate that LiveChannel depends on is already in place. The built-in email and community tools give associations ready distribution channels. And the ecosystem narrative is pre-sold: association executives reading YourMembership's own marketing are being told affinity programs are the path to non-dues revenue.

## Weaknesses / gaps
No native deals marketplace, no attribution, no commission reporting, no unique-code generator, no partner onboarding workflow, no standardized offer schema. Every association reinvents the wheel in a link list. There is no way for a vendor to publish one offer and have it syndicate across all YourMembership-using associations. This is the exact whitespace LiveChannel fills.

## LiveChannel implication
Adjacent, not competitive. YourMembership owns member identity and the portal surface but has no real deals layer — so LiveChannel can slot in as the structured offer-and-attribution system that YourMembership-using associations are clearly being nudged toward. Natural integration pattern: SSO from the YM member portal into a LiveChannel-powered perks page, with LiveChannel handling offer management, tracking, and commission share-back to the association as non-dues revenue.
